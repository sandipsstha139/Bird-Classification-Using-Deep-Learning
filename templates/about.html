Introduction

Welcome to our Bird Species Prediction Project! This project utilizes machine learning to predict the Endangered species of a bird based on the provided image.The list of Endangered bird species is collected from the Nepal's National Red List of Birds journal. The dataset for this project is collected from ebird website.

How It Works

Our model is trained using a dataset of 38 endangered bird species of Nepal. It leverages deep learning techniques to analyze and classify images of birds, providing accurate predictions of the species.
